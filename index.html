About the Author

My name is Shailendra Yadav, a 19+ year old aspiring writer from India. I’m new to the world of writing but deeply passionate about storytelling, creativity, and imagination. My journey as an author has just begun, and I’m excited to share my very first book with the world.

My debut novel, "ZERO AGE: The War of Eternal Balance", is a step into a new universe filled with mystery, war, and the struggle between light and darkness.Through my stories, I aim to transport readers into worlds beyond the ordinary and inspire them to think deeper.
